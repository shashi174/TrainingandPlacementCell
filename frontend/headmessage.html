Message from Training and Placement Cell

Dr. Arun Kumar Singh

In-Charge, Training & Placement Cell

The Training & Placement Cell of Rajkiya Enginnering College, Kannauj is established to facilitates the process of placement of students passing out from the Institudes as well as collaborating with industries and reputed organization in setting up of intership and training program of students.

It is a matter of pride and honor to extend you the invitation to participate in the Campus Recruitment Program of the Rajkiya Enginnering College. With an increasing thrust being placed by the All India Council for Technical Education and Government of India for skill development Industry Intersection, I am confident that your esteemed organization and Rajkiya Enginnering College, Kannauj stand to gain enormously from this symbolic relationship.

Established in the year 2015 by Government of Uttar Pradesh as an Associate College of Dr APJ Abdul Kalam Technical University, Rajkiya Engineering College is a new Technical Institution. The College is now affiliated to Dr APJ Abdul Kalam Technical University, Lucknow , and has the recognition of All India Council of Technical Education. Admissions to the institude take place through the State Entrance Examination (SEE). At Rajkiya Enginnering College, Kannauj, we take care to groom our students according to the needs of the industry. Our undergraduate students undergo eight-week training in their summer vacations in reputed industries/institutions/organizations, as part of their academic requirements. Please feel free to contact the Training & Placement Office should you require any further information/clarification.

Looking forward to a mutually beneficial relationship and with regards.
